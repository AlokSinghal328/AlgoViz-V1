About Us

What is AlgoViz?

AlgoViz is a free and open platform designed to help developers and students visualize algorithms and data structures. It simplifies complex logic using interactive animations, making learning more intuitive, visual, and engaging. Whether you're preparing for interviews or building your fundamentals, AlgoViz has you covered.

We Value Your Feedback

We'd love to hear your thoughts and ideas! At AlgoViz, we're dedicated to making learning algorithms and data structures as clear and interactive as possible. Your feedback helps us improve our visualizations, add new features, write better articles, and create helpful solutions for learners like you. Whether you have suggestions, spot an issue, or simply want to share what you love, your input is invaluable to us. Let's build AlgoViz together into the best learning platform it can be — your voice makes a difference!
