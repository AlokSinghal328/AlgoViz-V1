My Story

The Story and Vision
Behind AlgoViz

Dear Learner,

I'm Alok, and I built AlgoViz with one simple belief — that learning how code really works shouldn't feel overwhelming or confusing. I started this project as a learner myself. Like many of you, I spent countless hours watching tutorials and solving problems, yet sometimes struggled to see what was really happening behind each line of code.

That's when I realized something was missing — clear, step-by-step visualizations and practical explanations that break down complex algorithms into ideas we can actually understand and remember. So I decided to build it myself.

AlgoViz is my way of giving back to the community that helped me grow. Here, you won't just find code — you'll find dry runs, time and space complexity breakdowns, and interactive tools to see your logic come alive. My goal is to make AlgoViz your trusted learning partner — whether you're preparing for an interview, brushing up on core concepts, or simply exploring how things work under the hood.

Looking ahead, my vision is to expand AlgoViz into a space where anyone can learn, share, and master programming together — with clear explanations, practical solutions, and a community that grows with you.
Thank you for visiting this little corner I built for us. I hope it helps you learn better and feel more confident in your coding journey.

Keep learning, keep building.

— Alok Singhal
